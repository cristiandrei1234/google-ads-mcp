# monthly-client-report-pack

## Cadence
Monthly

## Objective
Deliver clear business narrative from media activity to outcomes.

## Workflow
- Build month-over-month KPI summary with budget, volume, efficiency, and quality context.
- Summarize top wins, losses, and decisions made during the month.
- Translate platform metrics into business-relevant insights for client stakeholders.
- Attach next-month priorities, tests, and expected outcomes.

## Core MCP Tools
- run_gaql_query
- list_campaigns
- get_change_history

## Expected Outputs
- Executive summary for client
- KPI appendix by campaign
- Next-month action roadmap

## Guardrails
- Keep narrative evidence-based and numerically consistent.
- Avoid vanity metrics without business link.
- Include open risks and dependencies.
