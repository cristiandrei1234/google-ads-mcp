# monthly-growth-opportunities

## Cadence
Monthly

## Objective
Generate and prioritize scalable growth initiatives with estimated upside.

## Workflow
- Run gap analysis across keywords, search terms, devices, geos, audiences, and assets.
- Estimate upside and effort for each opportunity.
- Prioritize opportunities into now/next/later roadmap.
- Provide implementation briefs for top opportunities.

## Core MCP Tools
- run_gaql_query
- get_search_terms
- list_keywords
- list_ads
- list_custom_audiences
- list_campaigns

## Expected Outputs
- Growth opportunity backlog
- Impact vs effort prioritization
- Implementation briefs

## Guardrails
- Do not prioritize opportunities without minimum data confidence.
- Separate quick wins from strategic bets.
- Respect business and compliance constraints.
